User Management & Access Control

"Our sophisticated user management system ensures that every team member has exactly the right level of access they need, and nothing more. From administrators with full system control to contractors with project-specific permissions."

"Role-based access control means users automatically inherit permissions based on their job function. Project managers can oversee their projects, contractors can only access assigned defects, and inspectors have read-only access for quality assurance."
