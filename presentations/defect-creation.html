Defect Creation Process Narration

"Creating a defect in our system takes just 5 minutes and ensures every critical detail is captured."

"Imagine you're walking through a construction site and you spot a defect - maybe a cracked foundation or faulty wiring. Instead of scribbling notes on a napkin, you pull out your phone or tablet."

"Our intuitive interface guides you through each step: name the defect, describe the issue, select the priority level, and choose the affected floor plan. The system automatically suggests recent descriptions to save you time."

"Then comes the magic - you upload multiple high-quality images, and with our visual floor plan integration, you can precisely pin the exact location of the defect. No more vague descriptions like 'somewhere on the second floor'."

"Hit submit, and boom - the defect is instantly created, notifications are automatically sent to the assigned contractor and project manager, and the issue enters our comprehensive tracking system."
